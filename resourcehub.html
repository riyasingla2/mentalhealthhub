    some questions to ponder on:

    • who do you lean on the most when you are spiralling? how do they make you feel comforted or reassured?
    • how can you take time to refocus your attention on yourself and your needs instead of everyone(and everything) else?
    • who has had the greatest positive impact on your life? have you told them?
    • what's something you are grateful for right now and why?
    • what's your happy place? how can you spend more time there today or in future?

    if you are feeling extra stressed today, help take off your mind of everything for a few moments by doing something creative like drawing, taking up a new hobby or just even journaling. also, in your head, say to yourself I am and take a big inhale, hold it and then exhale as you say enough.
